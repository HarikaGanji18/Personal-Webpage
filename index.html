"Echoes in the Mist"

"Echoes in the Mist" is a mysterious and atmospheric novel that weaves a tale of secrets, love, and the passage of time. Set in a small, fog-covered coastal town, the story follows a protagonist who returns to their childhood home after many years away. As they uncover hidden letters, old journals, they begin to piece together the town’s dark past and its connection to a series of unexplained disappearances.
