About My Journey

Welcome to my skincare journey! I've struggled with acne for years and have tried numerous products and routines. Here, I'll share what has worked for me, my progress, and tips for anyone on a similar path.

My Daily Skincare Routine

  • Morning: Cleanser, Vitamin C serum, Moisturizer, Sunscreen
  • Evening: Cleanser, Exfoliating toner (2-3 times a week), Retinol serum, Moisturizer

Consistency is key! I make sure to follow my routine every day to maintain healthy skin.
